# Decorate-the-Christmas-tree
Game: help a girl named Lucy decorate the Christmas tree
16-bit retro graphics.
Objective: help a girl named Lucy decorate the Christmas tree.
Game eements: 
a Christmas tree
Lucy (the NPC who gives instructions)
Christmas ornaments scattered around the room.

Mechanics: 
drag n drop: players must drag ornaments onto the tree.
Movement logic: regular ornaments can be placed freely on the branches, while the Star has a unique mechanic it must be precisely snapped to the top of the tree.
Feedback: successful placement makes Lucy happy (she triggers a "hands up" animation).
Ending: once all 9 items are on the tree, the scene transitions: it becomes evening, snow starts falling outside the window, and a pile of gifts appears under the decorated tree.
